Frequently Asked Questions

How does your site work?

Our website works the same way that other shopping websites online work. You can browse items through the website and add as many items as you would like. Shopping Bag will update automatically based on your selections. Click on Checkout at the top of the page once you finished your selection and complete your order.

How can I check out?

Customers can create an account with us to check out . Account creation will provides additional benefits like Order Tracking, Order History and Better Customer Service.

What forms of payments do you accept?

We accept all the major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover.

Is your site secure?

Our site is 100% secure. We use a variety of measures to maintain the privacy and safety of your personal information. Credit/Debit card usage is GUARANTEED SAFE. We do not store or save any credit information of our customers. Payment processing is completely handled by the Payment Gateways secure servers. Please visit our site security page for further details.

What is your shipping policy?

Please refer to our Shipping Policies page for detailed information on shipping and returns.

How long after my order is placed will it ship?

Orders are processed in the order they were received and also the shipping choice that was selected. Orders will be usually ready to be picked up with in 2 to 3 hours.

Are your products fresh?

We sell only Authentic Brand and Quality Products.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders cannot be cancelled or modified once it has been picked up. Orders can be changed or modified prior to the pickup by calling us at +1 660-528-0821

What is the return policy?

Please check our Shipping Policies page.
